About RPJ Software

RPJ Software is a Tech-Educational class for anyone and everyone who wants to learn and keep themselves updated with the latest Information Technology & Computer Science trends & Information. We are dedicated in providing high quality educational and informational content for everyone.

At Skillshare, we’ve seen again and again how the seemingly simple act of creating can be a force for growth, change, and discovery in people’s lives. We want to inspire and multiply the kind of creative exploration that furthers expression, learning and application.

Skillshare is an online learning community with thousands of classes for creative and curious people, on topics including illustration, design, photography, video, freelancing, and more. On Skillshare, millions of members come together to find inspiration and take the next step in their creative journey.
